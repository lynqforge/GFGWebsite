Privacy Policy

GFG Power ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service to monitor your systems (such as solar energy systems, batteries, or other connected devices). Our app and website are designed with privacy in mind and are fully compliant with Apple's App Store and Google's Play Store privacy requirements.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our monitoring and dashboard services:

  • Device and System Data: Information sent from your systems (e.g., solar generation, battery performance, other device metrics).

  • Account Information: Email address or user ID used to identify and authenticate you.

  • Usage Logs: Basic diagnostic logs used for service maintenance and support (not linked to individual user activity unless necessary for debugging).

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Display relevant system performance and metrics on your dashboard.

  • Notify you of performance events (e.g., battery charge alerts, system failures).

  • Maintain and improve the quality and security of our service.

  • We do not use your data for profiling, marketing, or resale purposes.

Data Privacy and Access

  • Your data is private and can only be accessed by you.

  • No other client can view or access your data.

  • System administrators and staff have limited access only as necessary for technical support, system maintenance, or diagnostics, and never for external use.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • We do not sell, lease, or share your data with third parties.

  • We may use third-party service providers (e.g., for secure cloud hosting or analytics) who are contractually bound to only process data on our behalf and according to strict privacy and security standards.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS/TLS)

  • Secure storage and access controls

  • Regular audits and vulnerability testing

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained for as long as you use the service. Upon account deletion or request, your data will be securely deleted within a reasonable timeframe.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access or receive a copy of your data

  • Request correction or deletion

  • Withdraw consent and stop data processing

  • To exercise these rights, please contact us at dev@gfgpower.us.

Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  1. Right to Know
    You have the right to request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources of that information, the purpose for collecting it, and whether it was disclosed and to whom.

  2. Right to Delete
    You have the right to request that we delete the personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., when the information is necessary to provide you with the service or comply with legal obligations).

  3. Right to Correct
    You may request correction of inaccurate personal information that we maintain about you.

  4. Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information
    We do not collect sensitive personal information as defined under CPRA, and therefore do not use or disclose such information beyond what is necessary for providing our service.

  5. Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing
    We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for any commercial purpose.

  6. Right to Non-Discrimination
    You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising your rights under the CCPA/CPRA.
